Arabic lessons for adults

For non-native speakers who want to actually speak Arabic — for travel, family, heritage, or work. Lessons are conversation-first and adapted to your level and goals.

How it works

1. Free intro call

A short, no-pressure call to understand your goals and current level.

2. A plan for you

Lessons built around what you actually want: everyday conversation, dialect, or classical Arabic (Fusha).

3. Regular practice

Weekly or biweekly one-on-one sessions in person, at a library or cafe convenient to you.

Who this is for

  • Complete beginners who want a real head start before a trip or move
  • Heritage speakers who understand some Arabic but want to speak more confidently
  • Professionals who need working Arabic for their job
  • Anyone who's tried apps and wants an actual conversation partner

Format & pricing

Lessons are one-on-one and in person, with single lessons and multi-lesson packages available. Rates depend on frequency and location — you'll get a clear, no-surprises quote in your free intro call.
